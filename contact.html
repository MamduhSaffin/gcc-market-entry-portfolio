Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for market entry?

From initial discussion to live marketplace presence typically takes 4-6 weeks, depending on product complexity and documentation.

What are the key requirements for GCC market entry?

Halal certification, product quality documentation, Arabic labeling, MOQ commitment, and regulatory compliance are essential.

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQ typically ranges from 1,000-5,000 units depending on product category and supplier agreements.

Which product categories perform best in GCC?

Premium wellness, collagen supplements, beauty products, and specialty foods show strongest consumer demand and loyalty.

How much support does eRomman provide?

Full support including compliance, Arabic localization, marketing guidance, logistics coordination, and customer service.

What are the market advantages for Malaysian brands?

Halal certification credibility, manufacturing quality reputation, production efficiency, and established regional trust.
